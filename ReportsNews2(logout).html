The “autumn star” adorns the dome of the sky starting today

Saturday ،17 September 2020 08:00 pM

WRITTEN BY : Fcis team



We show you our visitors the most important and latest news in the following article: “Autumn star” adorns the dome of the sky starting today, Saturday 19 September 2020 12:11 pm
In the sky of the Arab world, starting from this evening until next month, the star (the mouth of the whale), which is known as the “autumn star,” is observed in the sky of the Arab world, and it can be seen with the naked eye and is at its best at midnight. Engineer Majed Abu Zahra, head of the Astronomical Society in Jeddah, indicated, in a statement issued today, that (the mouth of the whale) is a bluish white star of the second magnitude, which is two times larger than the sun and 20 times brighter, is 25 light years away from Earth, and is the brightest star in a group The stars of Southern Pisces, which consist of seven stars, are arranged in an irregular hexagon.

He explained that this star is called by other names such as “the only star” because it is the brightest star that appears in an empty part of the sky, and it is also called “the lone star” or “the lone star.”

He said that in the first nights of the autumn season a star (the mouth of the whale) is observed, as it appears above the southeast horizon and there is no other bright star that is very low on the southeast horizon at this time of the year.

He added that with the passage of hours (the mouth of the whale) reaches its highest point above the southern horizon almost and then it sets in the southwest horizon almost, and the reason for this apparent movement of this star and the rest of the stars in the dome of the sky is due to the Earth’s rotation around its axis.

He stressed that this star (the mouth of the whale), in addition to being one of the brightest stars in the night sky, is very important, as it was discovered years ago that it has a rotating planetary disk, and this ring of dust surrounding the (mouth of the whale) may form planets someday.
